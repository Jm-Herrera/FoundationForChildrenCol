CULTURAL EVENTS AND SPECIAL MOMENTS

Our events are the highlight of all the hard work and dedication of the children. In each event, the young artists have the opportunity to showcase the talents they have developed in music classes, reading workshops, and creative activities. These gatherings not only allow parents and the community to celebrate the children's achievements but also inspire other families to join our mission. Through these events, we create a space where creativity, effort, and participation become a true source of pride and motivation.

In addition, every month we celebrate the children’s birthdays with a special party. The kids enjoy a delicious cake, songs, and a celebration that reminds them how important and unique they are to us. These celebrations not only create moments of happiness but also strengthen the community between the children, their families, and our team.

Valentine's Day - September Birthdays

September 14,2024

In Colombia, Love and Friendship Day is one of the most special festivities, celebrated on the third Saturday of September. This day is an opportunity for friends and loved ones to express their affection and appreciation, similar to Valentine’s Day in other countries. At our foundation, we join this tradition by organizing joyful activities where children share messages of friendship and participate in games to strengthen their bonds.

Our beginnings - Construction of the foundation's headquarters

May, 2023

The Foundation began its activities in 2022, but it was the following year when we started the construction and adaptation of our facilities. We would like to share with you images of this process, which represents the effort and commitment to our mission. The foundation is located in the Vencedores neighborhood, on the east side of the city of Villavicencio. Our facility is strategically positioned to allow easy access for children from the area and for families to easily reach us and participate in the artistic training programs we offer.
